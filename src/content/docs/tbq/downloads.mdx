## Award Requirements and Forms

![](/assets/2026/NMA_Requirements.jpg)

All of the material must be quoted one chapter at at time and in one sitting, with four mistakes or fewer. No time limit!

<LinkButton
    href="/assets/2026/GEPCP_NMA_Requirements.pdf"
    icon="seti:pdf"
    iconPlacement="start"
>
    National Memorization Award Requirements
</LinkButton>

![](/assets/2026/MMA_Requirements.jpg)

All of the material must be quoted consecutively in one sitting with 10 mistakes or fewer in 65 minutes or less.

<LinkButton
    href="/assets/2026/GEPCP_MMA_Requirements.pdf"
    icon="seti:pdf"
    iconPlacement="start"
>
    Master Memorization Award Requirements
</LinkButton>
