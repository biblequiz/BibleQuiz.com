## Venue

The National Bible Quiz Finals will be held at the beautiful [Loews Ventana Canyon Resort](https://www.loewshotels.com/ventana-canyon) in Tucson, AZ.

The room rate is only $145 per night for single, double, triple, and quadruple occupancy. The discounted rate applies for National Finals week, plus 3 days before and 3 days after.

<LinkButton href="https://www.loewshotels.com/ventana-canyon/group-national-bible-quiz-finals">
    Reserve your room
</LinkButton>

:::caution[Hotel Booking Deadline]
The discounted group rate closes on Monday, June 1, 2026. After that date, rooms will move to the regular rack rate of $258.00 per night for that week.
:::

Free parking is included.
