40 teams from 8 regions compete over the course of a week for the 2026 AG Teen Bible Quiz National Championship. 🏆

The National Bible Quiz Finals will be held at the beautiful [Loews Ventana Canyon Resort](https://www.loewshotels.com/ventana-canyon) in Tucson, AZ.

Check out [unique things to do in Tucson](./information/tucson/) while you are in town.

The dates are Saturday, June 27, through Friday night, July 3, 2026. The District Bible Quiz Coordinators Retreat will start at supper time on Friday night and go through noon on Saturday, June 27.

The room rate is only $145 per night for single, double, triple, and quadruple occupancy. The rates apply 3 days before and after National Finals.

Free parking is included.

The Resort Fee has also been waived and includes the following resort activities:

- Daily Guided Hike
- Daily Tennis Court Access
- Pool Games and Activities
- Pool Service and Pool Treats
- Resort Activities (Select Days/Nights)
- Star Gazing (Select Nights, Weather permitting)
- Premium Wi-Fi in guestrooms
- 45minute Ventana Tour (select days)
- Daily Transportation to Sabino Canyon and Ventana Village

Housing Link will open on Tuesday, April 28, 2026 and will be posted right here at BibleQuiz.com, along with complete schedules and other National BQ Finals information. Please check back regularly for updates.
