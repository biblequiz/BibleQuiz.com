The dates are Saturday, June 27, through Friday night, July 3, 2026.

## 1-on-1 Bible Challenge

Did you know if you are not part of the final 40 Bible Quiz teams at National BQ Finals you can still come and quiz as we are hosting the 1 on 1 Bible Challenge no matter what division you quizzed in all season. The material will be from all of Galatians, Ephesians, Philippians, Colossians and Philemon. Do you have what it takes to win the grand prize of a $1,000 BQ Scholarship. Think you have what it takes to come out on top? Step up, and show your mastery of the Bible!

Registration for this event will open May 18 after all Regional competitions are complete. Email [Pastor Bernie Elliot](pastorbernie@biblequiz.com) for more information.

## Scholarships

If you're in grades 6-12 and participated in the Championship Division of Teen Bible Quiz this season, you may qualify for one of several college scholarships. These are for students who've worked hard in Bible Quiz and are planning to pursue higher education after high school. Some are even open to students who didn't compete at National Finals.

Deadline to apply is June 8, and the recipients will be announced at National Finals.
