40 teams from 8 regions compete over the course of a week for the 2026 AG Teen Bible Quiz National Championship. 🏆

The dates are Saturday, June 27, through Friday night, July 3, 2026.

## Event Registration

All attendees must register, including quizzers, coaches, parents, and officials. Registration is open now and closes **May 20, 2026**.

## Scholarships

If you're in grades 6-12 and participated in the Championship Division of Teen Bible Quiz this season, you may qualify for one of several college scholarships. These are for students who've worked hard in Bible Quiz and are planning to pursue higher education after high school. Some are even open to students who didn't compete at National Finals.

Deadline to apply is June 8, and the recipients will be announced at National Finals.
