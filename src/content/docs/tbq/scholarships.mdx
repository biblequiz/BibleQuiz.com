Each year, over $10,000 is awarded in scholarships to various Bible Quiz participants. Additionally,
universities that recognize and appreciate the dedication of quizzers also extend scholarship opportunities.
Explore the list of available scholarships below.

Please note that scholarship details are subject to frequent updates. If you have any additional
opportunities or suggestions, we welcome you to reach out via email at 
[hello@biblequiz.com](mailto:hello@biblequiz.com).
